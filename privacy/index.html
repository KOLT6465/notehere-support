Privacy Policy

NoteHere

Effective July 16, 2026.

Summary

NoteHere does not collect, track, sell, transmit, or share personal data. It has no account, analytics, advertising, cloud service, or external network service.

Data Stored On Your Mac

NoteHere stores note text, note color, note position, creation and modification dates, and the exact webpage URL associated with each note. This data stays in Safari extension storage and the app's local shared container on your Mac.

Website Access

Safari website access lets NoteHere display its isolated note interface and identify the exact URL where you created a note. NoteHere does not inspect or store webpage content, form contents, passwords, cookies, or browsing history. Safari controls permission grants and revocation.

Exports And Deletion

Text is written outside the app only when you choose Export and select a destination. You can delete one note, every note on the current exact page, or all website notes from the manager.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through the NoteHere support page.
